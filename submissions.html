Submissions from first time authors

Step 1

Send via email and in word (.doc/.docx) format a synopsis (plus an outline in case of non-fiction) with a maximum of 250 words to synopsis@alphamensaepublishing.com

Step 2

Send via email and in word (.doc/.docx) format the first 3,000 words of your manuscript to samples@alphamensaepublishing.com only if our reviewing commission contacts you. Please indicate if your proposal is a multiple submission.

Step 3

Send via email and in word (.doc/.docx) format a copy of your entire manuscript (plus gray-scale and/or colour pictures if included) to manuscripts@alphamensaepublishing.com only if our reviewing commission contacts you.

Submissions from established authors

Step 1

Send via email and in word (.doc/.docx) format the first 3,000 words of your manuscript, your curriculum vitae and the title(s) of your published books to samples@alphamensaepublishing.com. Please indicate if your proposal is a multiple submission.

Step 2

Send via email and in word (.doc/.docx) format a copy of your entire manuscript (plus gray-scale and/or colour pictures if included) to manuscripts@alphamensaepublishing.com only if our reviewing commission contacts you.
