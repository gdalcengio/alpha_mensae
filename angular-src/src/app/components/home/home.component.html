Alpha Mensae Publishing Inc

Alpha Mensae is a small publishing house in operation since 2008. It is now open for submissions from accomplished and from first time authors. We offer standard trade contract royalties

Services

We assess, edit, copyright, print, market and promote mainly online

Interests

We focus primarily on the following genres:

  • Adventure
  • Biography
  • Fiction
  • Memoires
  • Native
  • Non-Fiction
  • Politics
  • Science Fiction
  • Social History
  • Teen
  • Western

Submissions

  1. Send via e-mail and in word (.doc/.docx) format a synopsis (plus an outline in case of non-fiction) with a maximum of 250 words to synopsis@alphamensaepublishing.com
  2. Send via email and in words (.doc/.docx) format the first 3,000 words of your manuscript to samples@alphamensaepublishing.com
  3. Send via email and in word (.doc/.docx) format your entire manuscript (plus gray-scale and/or colour pictures if included) to manuscripts@alphamensaepublishing.com
